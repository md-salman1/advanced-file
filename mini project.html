(FAQ) Section

1. How do I search for flights on your website?
Simply enter your departure and destination cities, along with your travel dates, into our search bar. We'll present you with a range of flight options that match your criteria.

2. Can I book flights for multiple passengers in one booking?
Absolutely! Our platform allows you to book flights for multiple passengers in a single transaction. Just select the number of travelers during the booking process.

3. What payment methods do you accept?
We accept a variety of payment methods, including major credit and debit cards. Select your preferred payment option during the checkout process.

4. Are the prices displayed on your website inclusive of all fees and taxes?
Yes, the prices you see include all applicable taxes and fees. There are no hidden charges, so you can make informed decisions.

5. Can I modify my booking after it's been confirmed?
Depending on the airline's policies, you may be able to make modifications to your booking.
